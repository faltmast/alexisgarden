Volta

Invitation only. Peripatetic. Built to recharge.

A volta is an invitation only peripatetic retreat organised by a small group to recharge, share knowledge, and plant seeds for good habits. I started Volta in 2018 with Moritz Bierling and Alex Beltechi as a platform to experiment with experiences and community building practices.

In many years of learning through doing we explored:

  • Meditating in ice
  • Hiking unknown paths
  • Photographing vulnerable moments
  • Sharing dreams
  • Learning to trust
  • Facing death
  • Making fire
  • Visualizing how the future can be
