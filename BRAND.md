## Typography

### Headers
**Font:** Inter or similar clean sans-serif
**Style:** Bold, large, generous letter-spacing for impact
**Treatment:** S P A C E D  C A P S for category labels

### Body
**Font:** System sans-serif (clean, fast-loading)
**Size:** 18-20px for readability
**Line-height:** 1.6-1.7

### Accents
**Font:** Serif or italic for soft descriptors ("of nature", "Made for")
**Usage:** Sparingly, for emphasis and warmth

### Scale
- Hero heading: 4-6rem (72-96px)
- Section heading: 2.5-3rem (40-48px)
- Subsection: 1.5rem (24px)
- Body: 1.125rem (18px)
- Small/labels: 0.875rem (14px)

## Visual Elements

### Texture
- Paper-like background (subtle grain/noise)
- Not sterile white â€” warm cream
- Organic, analogue feel

### Illustrations
- Hand-drawn brush strokes
- Ink wash / watercolor marks
- Simple iconography (like Tulum collection)
- Imperfect, human, not vector-perfect

### Iconography Style
- Single brush stroke abstractions
- Could represent the three pillars:
  - **Strategy:** Seed or compass mark
  - **Photography:** Eye or aperture stroke
  - **Experience:** Wave or breath mark

### Photography
- B&W for Alexis portrait
- Documentary style for work samples
- Full-bleed or contained with generous margins

---

## Layout Principles

### Whitespace
- Generous padding (80-120px sections)
- Let content breathe
- Apple-level restraint

### Grid
- Bento-style cards for services
- Asymmetric where it adds interest
- Single column for focused reading

### Hierarchy
1. Big bold statement
2. Supporting context
3. Action/link

### Motion
- Subtle, purposeful
- Gentle fades on scroll
- Nothing flashy
