What is Volta?

Volta is a collection of tools, frameworks, and resources designed to help founders navigate the complexity of building and scaling companies. From strategic thinking to operational execution, Volta provides practical guidance for the challenges you're facing right now.

What you'll find

Strategic Frameworks

Mental models and tools for making better decisions about positioning, product, and growth.

Practical Resources

Templates, checklists, and guides for common founder challenges from fundraising to team building.

Case Studies

Real examples of strategy in action, showing what worked, what didn't, and why.
